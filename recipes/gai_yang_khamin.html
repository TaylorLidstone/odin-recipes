Gai Yang Khamin Southern Thai Turmeric Chicken

Description:

A Southern Thai street food. Traditionally grilled over coals using butterflied whole chicken.

Ingredients

  • 5 large chicken thighs (bone-in)[can use 1.2kg of any type of chicken]
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 1/2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp white pepper
  • 1 tbsp ground turmeric
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar (packed)

Steps

  1. Mix all non-chicken ingredients in a large bowl. Then add chicken and toss to coat.
  2. Marinate for at least 3 hours.
  3. Place chicken on a baking paper lined tray, skin side up. Scrape all marinade out the bowl and put on chicken.
  4. Bake for 40 mins at 180 degrees (160 fan forced). Basting at the 30 and 40 minute mark using the juices.
  5. Increase temperature to 200 degrees (180 fan forced) and cook for 10 more minutes.
  6. Rest for 3 minutes and serve with jasmine or coconut rice.

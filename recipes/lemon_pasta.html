Lemon Pasta

Description:

Pasta with lemon - really simple but really tasty.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 packet of pasta
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp lemon juice (fresh)
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan

Steps

  1. Cook pasta with salt and then drain.
  2. Stir in butter, soy sauce, lemon juice and parmesan cheese.

Uova In Purgatorio

Description:

A traditional italian recipe that can be eaten at any time of the day.

A hearty dish of eggs in a garlic and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 3 black olives, pitted and chopped
  • 1 tsp capers
  • 1 anchovy fillet
  • 200g crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 sprig of parsley
  • Pinch of salt (to taste)
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a deep frypan
  2. Add garlic, olives, capers and anchovy. And cook for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes and water, bring to a boil and then simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Add parsley and season with salt.
  5. Gently add eggs into tomato sauce without breaking the yolks.
  6. Cook for 3-5 minutes.
